Two Styles of Higher Hangers

Velvet Coated Slimline

Velvet-coated Higher Hangers are perfect for garments that might slide off a typical clothes hanger like dresses, strappy shirts, pants and skirts. The velvet hangers also feature a slimline design that allows more hangers to fit next to one another in a given amount of space.

HDF Wood-Style

Of course, any shirt will work with velvet-coated Higher Hangers, but I personally recommend HDF for shirts that aren't prone to slip off like sleeved, collared, or dress shirts. For ultimate style, HDF Higher Hangers provide a clean look, and thicker hanging surface offering more shoulder support for your shirts. They're incredibly durable and will last a lifetime.
